This is Something

Historically, the fore-bearers of Obonoma are descents of the Benin Kingdom in the present Edo State of Nigeria. They had lived in part of Delta Edo of the ancient Benin Kingdom without hindrance until about 1440AD during the reign of Oba Ewuare (Awuare) the Great who was the 12th Oba of Benin Kingdom.

Obonoma is located in the region of Rivers. Rivers's capital Port Harcourt (Port Harcourt) is approximately 26 km / 16 mi away from Obonoma (as the crow flies). The distance from Obonoma to Nigeria's capital Abuja (Abuja) is approximately 488 km / 303 mi (as the crow flies).
